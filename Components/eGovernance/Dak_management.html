Correspondence/DAK Management

A Correspondence (or a DAK) Management System is a system used by organizations to manage and track the flow of different types of correspondence within the organization.

This system includes tools for creating and organizing digital documents, as well as software for tracking the status of documents as they move through various stages of review, approval, and distribution. DAK/File Management Systems also include features for automating workflows and setting up notifications or alerts for specific events or milestones.

The system can have the same generic workflows for all types of Correspondences or it can be Separate workflows for different types of Correspondences within an organization.
