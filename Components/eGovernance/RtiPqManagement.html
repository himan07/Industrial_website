RTI/PQ Management

RTI/PQ Management typically refers to the process of handling Right to Information (RTI) requests and Parliamentary Questions (PQs) within a government or public institution. Our RTI/PQ Management Software is designed to simplify this process, ensuring compliance, tracking, and seamless communication.

Some of the key benefits of our RTI/PQ Management software include:

  • Transparency:  Our software improves transparency and accountability by providing citizens with easy access to information and response tracking.
  • Efficiency:  It reduces manual paperwork and streamline the request handling process, saving time and resources.
  • Compliance:  The software ensures compliance with RTI regulations and deadlines, reducing the risk of legal issues.

Top Functionalities of our RTI/PQ Management Software:

  • Request Submission:  Our software allows citizens to submit RTI and PQ requests electronically through online forms or applications.
  • Request Tracking:  It provides requestors with the ability to track the status and progress of their requests in real-time.
  • Document Management:   It stores and manages RTI /PQ documents securely, including request letters, responses, and related records.
  • Automated Workflow:   Implement automated workflows to route requests to the appropriate departments or personnel for processing.
  • Request Prioritization:   Our RTI/PQ Management assigns priority levels to requests based on urgency or legal requirements.
