Vision AI

Vision AI is a field of artificial intelligence that focuses on enabling machines to interpret and understand visual information from the world around them. It involves the use of algorithms and models to analyze and extract meaningful information from digital images or videos.

Vision AI uses machine learning algorithms to recognize and categorize objects within an image or video , and to detect and track movements and changes within the visulal environment.

Some common application of Vision AI include:

  • Object detection and recognition : Identifying and categorizing objects with an image or video.
  • Facial recognition : Identifying and verifying individuals based on their facial features.
  • Image and video search : using visuals data to search for and retrive relevent images and videos.
  • Autonomous vehicles : using vision AI to identify defects or anomalies in products during the manufacturing process.
