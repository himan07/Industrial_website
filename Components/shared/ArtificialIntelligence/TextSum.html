Text Summarization

Text summarization is the process of using natural language processing techniques to automatically generate a shorter version of a longer text while retaining its key information and meaning.

Text summarization has numerous applications such as::

  • News summarization
  • Document summarization
  • Social media summarization
  • Automatic summarization

Our Text summarization software is exclusively developed for tasks such as information retrieval, content generation, and data analysis while retaining the essential information and key points.

This is how our software adds value to your business:-

Time Efficiency: The software quickly processes and summarizes large volumes of text, saving time for users who would otherwise need to read the entire document.

Information Retrieval: It helps locate relevant information within lengthy documents, making it easier to find key points and important details.

Content Curation:  The application curates and repurposes content for blogs, newsletters, and social media.

Data Analysis:  The software summaries to extract trends, insights, and patterns from extensive datasets and reports.

Key Features and Capabilities of our Text Summarization Software:-

Extractive and Abstractive Summarization:
Extractive Summarization selects and extracts existing sentences or phrases from the source text to form a summary. Whereas abstractive summarization generates summaries by paraphrasing and rephrasing content, often in a more human-like manner.

Automatic Document Summarization:
Our software can process and summarize lengthy documents, research papers, articles, and other forms of text quickly and efficiently.

Multi-document Summarization:
Our Text Summarization software is also capable of summarizing multiple documents on a similar topic, providing a consolidated overview.

Multi-lingual Support:
Offers the ability to summarize text in multiple languages, facilitating global use and accessibility.

Customizable Summary Length:
Allows users to specify the desired length or word count for the generated summary.

Content Extraction and Relevance Ranking:
Identifies and extracts the most relevant sentences or paragraphs for inclusion in the summary.
