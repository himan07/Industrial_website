Robotic Process Automation

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is a technology that uses software robots, or "bots", to automate repetitive and rule-based tasks that were previously done by humans. RPA bots can perform tasks such as data entry, invoice processing, and customer service, among others.

Our RPA bots are especially designed to mimic human actions by interacting with applications, databases, and other systems just as a human would. The bots can be programmed to perform tasks based on predefined rules and conditions,
